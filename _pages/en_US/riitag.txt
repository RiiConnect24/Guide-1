If you need help for anything regarding this tutorial, please join [the RiiConnect24 Discord server](https://discord.gg/b4Y7jfD) (recommended) or [e-mail us at support@riiconnect24.net](mailto:support@riiconnect24.net).
{: .notice--info}

RiiTag is a customizable and dynamic gamertag. By sharing your gamertag (a
dynamic image), you can show what you've been playing to your friends! You need a Discord account in order to start using RiiTag.

### Choose a console to use with RiiTag
* [Wii](riitag-wii) - Available through the use of USB loaders.
* [Wii U](riitag-wiiu) - Available through the use of a plugin for the Wii U
  Plugin System (WUPS).
